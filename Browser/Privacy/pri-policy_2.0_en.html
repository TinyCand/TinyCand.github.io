Privacy Notice (GDPR) – EcoSight Browser

Version: 2.0.0
Last updated: 2026-06-13
Effective date: 2026-06-13

1. Who We Are (Controller)

This Privacy Notice explains how EcoSight Browser (the “App”) processes personal data. The App is developed by TinyCand. For GDPR purposes, the “controller” is the entity that determines how and why your personal data is processed.

Controller contact: TinyCand
Email: tinycand@outlook.com

2. Scope

This Privacy Notice applies to the EcoSight Browser mobile application and related services. It does not cover third-party websites you visit through the App.

3. Categories of Personal Data We Process

3.1 App usage and diagnostics (Firebase)

When you use the App, we may process limited information to understand how the App is used and to improve stability and performance. Depending on your choices, this may include:

  • App interaction events (e.g., screens viewed, feature usage, button taps).
  • App and device information (e.g., app version, OS version, device model, language).
  • Identifiers associated with your device or app installation (e.g., Firebase installation identifiers; and, if enabled on your device, advertising identifiers).
  • Approximate location inferred from network signals (country/region level), where supported by the platform/SDK.

3.2 Content of web pages you browse

The App is a browser. The web pages you visit are served by third-party websites. We do not need to collect the content of the pages you browse to provide the App’s core functionality. However, like most browsers, the App may process URLs and page information locally in order to load content, manage tabs, and provide browser features.

3.3 Data we do not collect as part of analytics

We do not collect, store, or transmit the following types of information for analytics purposes:

  • Browsing history (the list of websites you visit).
  • Search queries you enter.
  • Passwords, payment details, or form contents.
  • The content of the web pages you view.

Third-party websites you visit may collect data independently (for example, cookies, trackers, and logs) under their own privacy policies.

3.4 App permissions

The App may request the following permissions, depending on your use:

  • Internet: required for web browsing.
  • Notifications: used to show playback controls for text-to-speech (TTS).
  • Foreground service: used to keep TTS playback running reliably.
  • Clipboard access: used to support paste/copy actions; processed locally.
  • Microphone / Camera: used only when a website requests them (for example, voice/video features in web pages) and you grant permission.
  • Location: used only when a website requests location and you grant permission.

4. Purposes and Legal Bases (GDPR)

We process personal data only when we have a legal basis. Typical bases include:

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) – for optional analytics/measurement where required by law (e.g., in the EEA/UK/Switzerland). You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) – for basic security, fraud prevention, and maintaining the App, where these interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)) – to comply with applicable laws or lawful requests from authorities.

If you are located in the EEA/UK/Switzerland and we request consent for analytics, we will ask before enabling such processing and provide an option to change your choice later.

5. Firebase (Google) and Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (a service provided by Google) to help us understand how the App is used and to improve features and performance.

  • Provider: Google Ireland Limited (for users in the EEA/UK) and/or Google LLC (depending on your location and service arrangement).
  • Processing: Google acts as a service provider/processor for analytics data collected via Firebase Analytics.
  • Consent management (where applicable): we may use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage consent choices required by law.
  • More information: Firebase Privacy and Security and Google Privacy Policy.

If your jurisdiction requires consent, we will use your choice to control analytics data collection. Where supported, we may also apply consent signals to Google technologies (for example, consent mode/consent settings for analytics storage) consistent with your choice.

6. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share personal data in the following situations:

  • Service providers – such as analytics providers (e.g., Firebase/Google) that help us operate, maintain, and improve the App.
  • Legal and safety – where required to comply with law, enforce our terms, or protect users and the public.
  • Business transfers – in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (we will provide notice where required).

7. International Data Transfers

Firebase/Google may process data on servers located outside your country, including in the United States and other jurisdictions. Where GDPR applies, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) as required.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Notice, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Analytics retention is also influenced by settings in Firebase (for example, a configured retention window). You may request deletion where applicable.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR and other laws, including the right to:

  • Access your personal data and obtain a copy.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Delete personal data (where applicable).
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Data portability (where applicable).
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent).

You can typically control analytics consent from within the App’s privacy settings (if available) and through your device settings (for example, advertising identifier controls).

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe our processing violates applicable law.

10. Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11. Children

The App is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to provide valid consent for data processing. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us, please contact us.

12. Changes to This Privacy Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date and, where required, provide additional notice or request consent.

13. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: tinycand@outlook.com
